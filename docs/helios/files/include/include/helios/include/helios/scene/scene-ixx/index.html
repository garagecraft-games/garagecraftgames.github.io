24 /**
25 * Represents a hierarchical SceneGraph that organizes all renderable
26 * and non-renderable objects within a scene.
27 *
28 * Every scene has one implicit root node all nodes are appended to when
29 * `addNode` is called.
30 *
31 * A Scene is responsible for managing the SceneGraph, consisting of SceneNodes;
32 * it provides methods to frustum cull SceneNodes based on a specific `FrustumCullingStrategy`
33 * and a particular `Camera` and also allows for propagating world transformations
34 * through the contained subtrees.
35 *
36 *
37 * @see [RTR, pp. 828]
38 * @see [Gre19, pp. 693]
39 * @see [HDMS+14, pp. 138]
40 *
41 * @note By default, we're not allowing copying/moving Scenes.
42 * These constraints may be relaxed in the future, depending on valid use cases.
43 */
